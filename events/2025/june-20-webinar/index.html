About This Webinar

This webinar will focus on how to respond to the changing data landscape that is likely to affect food and agricultural research. Trusted data on food and agriculture are essential for high quality research and decision-making.

Join leading experts as they discuss the future of food and agricultural data, exploring innovative approaches to developing a new roadmap for trusted data systems that support evidence-based policy making in agriculture and food security.

What You'll Learn

  • Current challenges in the food and agricultural data landscape
  • Strategies for developing trusted data systems in agriculture
  • Best practices for data quality and reliability in agricultural research
  • Future directions for food and agricultural data infrastructure
  • Policy implications of trusted agricultural data systems

Who Should Attend

This webinar is designed for:

  • Agricultural researchers and food scientists
  • Policy makers in agriculture and food security
  • Data managers in agricultural organizations
  • Government officials working with agricultural data
  • Students and academics in agricultural and food policy
  • Anyone interested in trusted data systems for agriculture
