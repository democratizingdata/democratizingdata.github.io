Event Overview

The Harvard Data Science Review Special Issue webinar series brought together leading experts, researchers, and practitioners to explore the critical topic of democratizing data. Over two comprehensive days, participants engaged with cutting-edge research, practical implementation strategies, and innovative approaches to data discovery and utilization.

This event featured presentations from distinguished speakers across academia, government agencies, and industry, covering topics from data value creation to search methodologies, practical implementation lessons, and building collaborative communities around data democratization.
