Frequently Asked Questions

Common questions about our initiative, tools, and how to get involved.

How can I access your data discovery tools?

Our tools are freely available through our website. Visit our Tools section to explore dashboards for different federal agencies and datasets. No registration is required for basic access.

Can I contribute data or research to your initiative?

Absolutely! We welcome contributions from researchers, institutions, and organizations. Contact us to discuss how your data or research can be integrated into our platform and benefit the broader community.

Is there a cost to use your services?

Our core tools and resources are free to use. We believe in democratizing access to data, which means removing financial barriers. Some advanced features or custom implementations may involve collaboration discussions.

How do you ensure data quality and accuracy?

We work directly with federal agencies and use rigorous validation processes. Our machine learning algorithms are continuously refined, and we maintain transparency about our methodologies and data sources.

Can I use your tools for commercial purposes?

Our tools are designed primarily for research, education, and public benefit. For commercial applications, please contact us to discuss appropriate usage and any necessary agreements.
