About Democratizing Data

The Democratizing Data Initiative brings together leading technology companies, research institutions, and federal agencies to advance the discovery and utilization of public data assets.

Collaborators

Our initiative represents a strategic partnership between leading research institutions and technology companies specializing in data infrastructure, research analytics, and federated data ecosystems.

National Data Platform

Lead: Manish Parashar

NSF-funded initiative (NSF 2333609) building the nation's federated data ecosystem, promoting collaboration, innovation, and equitable use of data. Partners include San Diego Supercomputer Center (SDSC), UC San Diego, SCI Institute, University of Utah, University of Colorado Boulder, and EarthScope Consortium.
