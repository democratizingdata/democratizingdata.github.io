Advancing the Nation's Federated Data Ecosystem

The Democratizing Data Initiative is proud to partner with the National Data Platform (NDP) in building the nation's federated data ecosystem. This collaboration represents a significant step forward in our mission to democratize access to public data assets and promote innovation through data-driven research.

What is the National Data Platform?

The National Data Platform is a federated and extensible data ecosystem funded by NSF 2333609 under CI, CISE Research Resources programs. It promotes collaboration, innovation, and equitable use of data on top of existing cyberinfrastructure capabilities.

Key Capabilities:

  • Explore AI services and data collections - Search through rich collections of open data and AI services spanning diverse applications
  • Use and create data and AI services - Dive into data within your browser and run analyses on scalable computing platforms
  • Learn AI through data projects - Join educational activities and practice AI skills using real-world data
  • Host educational activities - Support classroom teaching with applicable real-world data projects

Our Collaborative Vision

This partnership combines Democratizing Data's expertise in data usage analysis and discovery with NDP's federated infrastructure capabilities. Together, we are working to:

Enhance Data Discoverability

Integrate our machine learning algorithms for dataset identification with NDP's comprehensive data catalog to improve how researchers find and access federal data assets.

Promote Educational Innovation

Support AI education through data-driven projects that help students and researchers understand how government data contributes to scientific advancement.

Scale Research Infrastructure

Leverage NDP's scalable computing platforms to provide broader access to our data usage analytics and dashboard capabilities.

Foster Open Science

Advance open science principles by making data usage insights and research discovery tools more accessible to the broader scientific community.

Impact and Reach

The National Data Platform currently serves:

  • 9 data collections and livestreams spanning diverse research domains
  • 5 data and AI services providing scalable computing and analysis capabilities
  • 421+ registered users from academic institutions, research organizations, and federal agencies
