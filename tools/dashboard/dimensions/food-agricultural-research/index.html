About This Platform

The Search and Discovery Platform for Food and Agricultural Research Datasets emerged from a multi-year collaboration with agricultural researchers, New York University, and the US Department of Agriculture. It is consistent with calls for dataset search and discovery platform in the final reports produced by the Advisory Committee on Data for Evidence Building and the National AI Research Resources Taskforce.

Details are available on the Resources tab on this website. The source publication corpus for the Platform is the Dimensions database for the years 2017-2024. The specific search strategy is described in the technical report.
